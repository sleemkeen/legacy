About Kons

Kons Construction is a leader in the construction of commercial, industrial and residential real estate of Class A in Russia.

Since the foundation of the company, the turnover of the company has doubled steadily every year, the number of employees proportionately grows. Today, Kons Construction has more than 4,000 professionals. The company operates in Russia, CIS countries and Europe.
